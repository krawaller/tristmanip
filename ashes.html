  1. Eddie van der Woodsen says:

    Well, I must say that Ashes until now is my favourite Tristania album, I can tell that this is a masterpiece!
    Heavy songs mixed with strong and deep lyrics plus violent grunts and a beutiful voice.
    Einar did such a great job in this album with the piano, specially on The Gate (which is my favourite song on the album).
    In general, I think that Tristania took a jump in the dark with Ashes, ’cause on the previous album World of Glass the band was making songs with electronic elements and a very industrial influence, and then 4 years later they came with a completly organic and pure album, sounding much heavier, much stronger and talking about much more darker human feelings!

    So, Ashes is my favourite album of all the times and I must say that it was an important influence on my ‘musical life’.
    I’m so grateful for the whole band for doing such a good remedy to my soul and my mind, and I know that more precious stuff are coming next, Rubicon will be one of them!
